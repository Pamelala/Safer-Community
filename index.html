When a home is burglarized, the risk of further victimization is also increased for properties nearby. The risk of re-victimization starts a very short time after the initial burglary. Crime records indicate 25% of repeat burglaries occur within 7 days of the initial crime and 51% occur within 30 days, and declining over the next few months.
